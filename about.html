About Me

I am a visual artist exploring the boundaries between the tangible and the conceptual. My work is born from deep observation of the world around us and the constant search for new forms of expression.

Each piece is an investigation, a dialogue between materials, ideas and emotions. My artistic practice embraces multiple mediums, from traditional painting to contemporary installations.

Art is my language to communicate the invisible, to make the intangible tangible. Through photography, I capture fleeting moments that reveal deeper truths about our human experience and connection to place.

Based between New York and Buenos Aires, I draw inspiration from the energy and stories of these vibrant cities, always seeking to bridge cultures through visual narrative.
